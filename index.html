Generate a Maze!


Prim's algorithm creates a minimum spanning tree on a weighted graph. Here we use it to generate a maze.


After choosing a starting cell, we add all of the cell's neighbor's to a queue, shown in green. As they are added to the queue , the "link" between the starting cell is assigned a random weight. The maze is then extended to the cell with the lowest weighted connection to the maze. This process continues until the queue is empty.
